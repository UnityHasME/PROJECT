The unity of strong women is the power of equal opportunities

Our community is a place for everyone who strives for gender equality and elimination of discrimination based on gender. We believe that all people should have equal opportunities and rights, regardless of their gender, orientation or identity. We recognize the importance of women, girls and all other people being able to access education, work, health care and political participation without discrimination. In our community, we support each other and share knowledge and experience to fight together for gender equality. If you share our values and strive to achieve gender equality, then our community is open to you.
